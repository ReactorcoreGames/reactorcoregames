Reactorcore — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This policy applies to all projects, tools, websites, and automated services created and operated by Reactorcore — including games and mods on itch.io, content on Patreon, Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee, DeviantArt, YouTube, and any automated posting or publishing tools.

No Data Collection

None of Reactorcore's projects or tools collect personal data from visitors or users. There are no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no user accounts, no stored form submissions, and no server-side infrastructure under Reactorcore's control that collects or stores your information.

No Third-Party Tracking

Reactorcore does not embed third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data-collection services in any of its projects. Links and buttons pointing to external platforms (itch.io, Patreon, YouTube, etc.) are plain hyperlinks — following them takes you to those platforms under their own privacy policies.

Automated Posting Tools

Reactorcore uses automated tools to schedule and publish content to social media and content platforms. These tools post exclusively to Reactorcore's own accounts. They do not read, collect, store, or share any data about you or any other third-party users.

As a solo developer managing games, mods, tools, and art across many platforms simultaneously, automated posting is a practical necessity — not a spam strategy. Posts are curated, limited in frequency, and aimed at reaching people who would genuinely enjoy the content.

Third-Party Platform Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser user agent, referring URL) as part of normal hosting operations. See GitHub's privacy statement for details. Content hosted on itch.io, Patreon, and other platforms is subject to those platforms' own privacy policies.
